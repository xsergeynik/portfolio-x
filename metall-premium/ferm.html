LLC "Premium" offers its clients the production of metal trusses. We manufacture metal structures for our clients in any volume. The company's production facilities allow us to deliver products quickly and without defects. For truss production, pipes with cross-sectional dimensions ranging from 15×15 cm to 45×45 cm are used. They are made of high-quality steel. Therefore, the metal structures are highly reliable and durable.

Advantages of Metal Trusses

  • • Structural resistance to deformations
  • • Excellent anti-corrosion properties, as stainless steel is used for truss production
  • • Ability to create complex shapes and decorative finishes (painting)
  • • Low cost

Structural Calculation

Before manufacturing the metal structure, its length is calculated. The truss’s slope angle is taken into account. The height of the structure depends on the type of coverage and the weight of the metal structure.
At the design stage, it is calculated how much weight the truss must support, as panels (canopy) will be laid on the truss.
If the truss length exceeds 36 meters, a construction lift is calculated.

Calculations are performed using specialized software. Manual calculations are not recommended due to the high risk of errors. All calculations should be carried out by an experienced engineer.
The engineer will take into account all the specific features of the metal structure: span length, load on the truss, its angle, and the placement of the coverings.
